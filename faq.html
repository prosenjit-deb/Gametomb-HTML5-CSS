What is Game Tomb?

Game Tomb is the hassle-free game discovery portal that connects content creators and games media with developers and publishers. It’s a free and easy service for content creators and media to discover new games and share these experiences on their channels. Content creators and media can keep track of their requests for game keys all in one place whilst streamlining the process for our clients. Whilst clients get to display their games on our virtual shop window to an ever-growing network of global content creators and media.
